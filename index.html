3D Art

What is 3D art?

3D art is creating art digitally using software such as Blender, Unity or Maya. These software help you to model and mold shapes to your liking. 3D modelling is often used in games to create the landscape or objects within the game.

My Journey with 3D art

I have been creating my own 3D art for 2 years now and have enjoyed learning new techniques and starting new projects. I have found that whenever I am working on a new personal project I feel a deep sense of peace but also excitement, I love being able to see how something starts and comparing it to the finished result. To start my journey, I took part in the "right of passage" for every 3D artist, which is creating your very own donut. This was introduced by a very prominent figure in the Blender community called "Blender Guru" and is used to help beginners learn the basics of using Blender.
